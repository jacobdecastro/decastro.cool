IDS Editor-in-Chief

Ever since I got my start at the IDS as a page designer, I’ve had a love for design — both print and digital. I refuse to believe that print is dead. Print is evolving into a different product. This semester, the IDS reduced printing from twice-weekly to weekly. This would feel like a cut to most people. We saw it as an opportunity.

Managing Editor of Digital and Digital Editor

Developed new longform story template As the special publications editor during the spring 2018 semester, I began cleaning up our longform stories template and adapting it for guides. By the end of the semester, I finished the overhaul of the look. The new longform template, the foundation of every IDS buildout since the end of the fall 2018 semester, has a clean design, readable typography and is easily customizable for developers to adapt to their needs.

Created and co-hosted a weekly podcast, “Posted” Modeled after other news podcasts, “Posted” took a look at the top IDS stories throughout the week and featured an interview with an IDS reporter, editor or source. I also devloped the logo for the podcast and the overall IDS podcasts branding.
